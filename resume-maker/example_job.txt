About Stripe
Stripe is a financial infrastructure platform for businesses. Millions of companies—from the world's largest enterprises to the most ambitious startups—use Stripe to accept payments, grow their revenue, and accelerate new business opportunities. Our mission is to increase the GDP of the internet.

About the Role
We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our Platform team, which owns the core infrastructure that all of Stripe's products are built on. You'll work on systems that handle billions of API requests per month with 99.999% uptime.

- Partner with product teams to define and build new platform capabilities
- Drive technical strategy and architecture decisions with long-lasting impact
- Mentor engineers across the company; establish and improve engineering standards
- Lead incident response and reliability improvements
- Collaborate cross-functionally with Product, Security, and Data teams
